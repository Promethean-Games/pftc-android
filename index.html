Privacy Policy

Promethean Games LLC · Effective October 23, 2025

Information We Collect

Par for the Course is a local-first application. All game data (player names, scores, settings) is stored exclusively on your device using browser localStorage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Device Permissions

The Table Leveler feature uses your device's accelerometer/gyroscope sensor (DeviceOrientation API). This data is processed locally and never transmitted. No camera, microphone, or location access is required.

Purchases

Full-game unlock purchases are processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not store payment card information. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing. Purchase status is stored locally on your device.

Data Deletion

To delete all app data, tap the button below. This will remove all saved games, settings, and purchase records from your device.
